Photo Guide

    DISTINCTIVE AND BOLD

    Here are some guidelines in order to get the best outcome from your print:

  • Whether it's pets or humans (or both!), natural lighting always works best. So not under heavy lights or in direct sunlight!
  • Eye level photos always work best
  • No heavy filters - in order for the print to look as realistic as possible, stay away from filtered pictures!

    • Merged photos

  • All photos should be in similar lighting
  • Angles of photos should be the same (all at eye level etc.)
  • I understand that most merged photos are normally made up of at least one older photo - if this is the case and you're in doubt, please email me to check if the picture is suitable. In most cases, I am able to edit the photo to bring the quality up or make it brighter!

    • I am only an email away - so please message me if you are unsure of a photo or if you have any other queries!

    If you are unsure on a photo - please message me before ordering and we can make sure its suitable!
